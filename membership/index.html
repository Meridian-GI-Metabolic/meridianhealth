What it includes

Care that does not begin and end at the consultation door.

Patients on most metabolic pathways need more than an episode of treatment. They need continuity, periodic review, adjustment of medication, and clinical reading that arrives at the right moment in care. Meridian Membership is designed for that arc.

Membership is opened at the recommendation of a Meridian physician or surgeon. It is a clinical service, not a subscription product.

Membership

Physician-led, twelve-month arc.

Clinical inclusions

Six elements of care, held in one programme.

No. 01Physician review

Quarterly consultation with a consultant physician. Adjustment of medication, review of bloods, escalation if needed.

No. 02GLP-1 therapy

Where clinically appropriate, prescribed and titrated under physician supervision. Side-effect management included.

No. 03Dietetic input

Sessions with an Accredited Practising Dietitian, scheduled to your phase of care.

No. 04Psychology

Registered psychologist available for behavioural support, scheduled when clinically indicated.

No. 05Patient app

The Meridian app, with reading, trackers, and direct messaging to your care team between visits.

No. 06Shared care

Letters to your GP after every review. Clinical decisions remain visible and continuous.

Who it is for

Patients who need continuity, not a single episode of care.

Membership suits patients on a long medical weight-management arc, patients maintaining results after bariatric surgery, and patients managing insulin resistance, type 2 diabetes or related metabolic conditions where periodic specialist review is clinically indicated.

Membership is not appropriate for every enquiry. A Meridian physician will determine whether the programme is the right fit at first consultation, or whether a different pathway, surgical or medical, is more appropriate.

A referral is helpful but not required to enquire. Medicare rebates apply where the patient holds a valid GP referral and the service is eligible.

How it works

Three steps to enrolment.

01

Initial consultation

You meet a Meridian physician. The clinical question is examined in full, history, investigations and any current medication.

02

Pathway recommendation

If membership is the right pathway, the physician explains the programme, the medication plan, the review schedule and what is expected of you.

03

Twelve-month arc

Care unfolds across the year. Quarterly physician review. Dietetic and psychology input as required. The patient app holds the file in one place.

Diagnostics

Investigations held in the file, not chased between providers.

Periodic blood work, body composition, lipid and HbA1c monitoring, and other investigations clinically indicated for the medication and the patient. Pathology is requested through the practice and returns into the patient's clinical file.

Where additional imaging or specialist investigation is required, the practice arranges it within Brenan Place or with established referral partners.

Escalation

If the medical pathway reaches its limit, the surgical pathway is in the next room.

Some patients on the metabolic pathway will, over time, become candidates for bariatric surgery. The decision is reached through the same multidisciplinary review that opens the membership, and the surgical team is already known to the patient.

The transition is held within one practice. There is no second referral, no new file, no repetition of history.

Bariatric integration

For post-bariatric patients, membership holds the years that follow.

Bariatric surgery is a clinical event. The decade after is the patient's. Meridian Membership is open to post-bariatric patients of the practice for ongoing physician review, dietetic support, supplement monitoring, and management of weight regain or metabolic drift.

Care is coordinated with the operating surgeon and shared with the patient's GP.

Continuity

The same clinicians, the same file, every visit.

Membership is held by named clinicians. The physician who opens the programme is the physician who reviews you across the year. The dietitian and psychologist remain consistent. The patient app holds the longitudinal record.

Membership can be paused or closed at any time. Closure does not affect the patient's standing with the practice or any active surgical care.

Held to clinical standards

Meridian Membership is operated by Meridian Health under the supervision of consultant physicians.

It is not a wellness subscription, a coaching product, or a direct-to-consumer medication service.
