05
Medicare Rebates

Eligible consultations may attract Medicare rebates with a valid GP referral.

This allows patients to access specialist-led care while reducing out-of-pocket costs.

Patients receive:

Specialist physician reviews
Ongoing monitoring
Shared care communication with their GP

All within a structured programme.
