Meridian Health — Pre-Launch One Page Website

Build a one-page pre-launch website for Meridian Health.

This site will be deployed directly from Netlify as a temporary pre-launch destination while the broader website ecosystem is completed.

The objective is to introduce Meridian as a specialist clinic providing upper gastrointestinal, bariatric and metabolic care.
