COPYWRITING / AI LANGUAGE RESTRICTIONS

The website copy must feel human, restrained, editorial and institutionally credible.

Avoid generic AI-generated language patterns, inflated transitions and startup-style phrasing.

The writing should feel:

Clear
Direct
Calm
Structured
Specific
Human-edited
Minimal

Avoid verbose filler sentences and generic positioning language.

DO NOT USE GENERIC AI STRUCTURE PATTERNS

Avoid:

Excessive em dashes
Overuse of “while”
Overuse of “designed to”

Generic introductory framing

Avoid paragraphs that sound generated rather than authored.
