What is included

Everything in the programme, upfront.

  • Physician review, quarterlyFour consultations per year with the same AHPRA-registered consultant physician. Bloods reviewed, medication titrated, escalation managed.

Is this for me?

You may be right for this programme if:

Membership is offered following a physician assessment, not as a direct sign-up. These indicators will help you decide whether to enquire.

  • You have been managing excess weight or metabolic disease and have not had consistent clinical oversight.
  • You are interested in physician-assessed GLP-1 therapy and want it supervised properly, not prescribed online without monitoring.
  • You have had bariatric surgery and need structured long-term follow-up, nutritional monitoring, or management of metabolic drift.
  • You are managing type 2 diabetes, insulin resistance, or elevated metabolic markers that require regular clinical review.
  • You want a single clinical team who knows your file, rather than a different provider at every appointment.
A GP referral is helpful but not required to enquire. Your physician will determine the right pathway at your first consultation.
