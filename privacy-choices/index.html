Privacy choices

Your privacy choices.

The choices you have over your information in the Meridian patient app, including how to request deletion of your data.

Last updated 16 June 2026.

In short

You are in control of your information.

You can ask us to delete your data, stop using the app at any time, and choose whether to use the app’s optional features. For full detail on how your information is handled, see the privacy policy.

Your choices

What you can choose.

Request deletion of your data

You can ask us to delete the information held for you in the app. Contact the practice using the details below and quote your patient number. We will action your request and confirm once it is done. Deleting app data has no effect on your clinical care.

Pause or stop using the app

You can stop using the app at any time. Removing the app from your device removes the local copy of your information. Your clinical care is not affected, and you can sign in again later if you wish.

Update your information

If your details are out of date or incorrect, contact the practice and we will update them for you.

Choose whether to use optional features

The food-photo and nutrition-estimate features in the calorie tracker are optional. If you prefer not to send a photo or description to the third-party processing service that estimates nutrition information, you can choose not to use those features.

Marketing and advertising

We do not use your information for marketing or advertising, and we do not share it with advertisers. There is nothing you need to opt out of.

Questions about your choices

If you are not sure which choice is right for you, or you would like help making a request, contact the practice and we will guide you through it.

Make a privacy choice

Contact the practice.

To request deletion of your data or to make any other privacy choice, contact the practice during business hours. We will respond within one working day.
