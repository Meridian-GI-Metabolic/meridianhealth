Enquire about enrolment
Who it is for

Membership is recommended, not sold. Here is who it suits.

Membership is opened on the recommendation of a Meridian physician following an initial consultation. It is not available as a direct sign-up. The clinical assessment ensures the programme aligns with your needs before enrolment.

  • 01You are managing excess weight or metabolic disease and require ongoing physician oversight rather than a single appointment.
  • 02You are a candidate for, or have been prescribed, GLP-1 therapy and require supervised titration and monitoring.
  • 03You have had bariatric surgery and require long-term follow-up, nutritional monitoring, or management of metabolic drift.
  • 04You are managing type 2 diabetes, insulin resistance, NAFLD, or related metabolic conditions requiring structured clinical review.
  • 05You want continuity with a single clinical team rather than fragmented care across multiple providers.

A GP referral is helpful but not required for initial enquiry. Your physician will determine whether Membership is the appropriate pathway at your first consultation.

Questions

Common questions about Membership.

Do I need a GP referral to enquire?

No. You can contact the practice directly. A referral is helpful and may allow Medicare rebates to apply to eligible consultations, but it is not required to make an initial enquiry or to attend a first consultation.

Can I join Membership without being an existing Meridian patient?

Yes. Your pathway begins with an initial consultation with a Meridian physician. If Membership is the appropriate programme, enrolment is offered at or following that appointment.

Does Membership include GLP-1 medication costs?

No. GLP-1 medication is prescribed by your physician but dispensed via a pharmacy and is not included in the programme fee. Your physician will guide you through medication options and costs at your initial consultation.

What happens if I need surgery during the programme?

The surgical team at Meridian is in the same practice. If your physician and the multidisciplinary team determine surgery is the right next step, the transition is managed internally. No new referral, no new file, no repeat history.

Can Membership be paused or discontinued?

Yes. Membership can be paused or concluded at any point. Your clinical record and relationship with the practice remain intact. Active surgical follow-up, if applicable, continues independently of Membership status.

Are Medicare rebates available?

Medicare rebates may apply to physician consultations, dietitian sessions, and psychology sessions where eligible services are provided under a valid referral or mental health care plan. The practice can advise on applicable rebates at the time of enrolment.
