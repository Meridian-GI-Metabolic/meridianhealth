Privacy

Privacy policy.

How the Meridian patient app collects, uses and protects your information.

Last updated 16 June 2026.

In short

Your record stays inside the practice.

The Meridian patient app is operated by Meridian Health. The information you enter sits within the practice’s clinical system and is used only to support your care. It is not sold, shared with advertisers or used for any purpose outside your care.

The detail

What we collect and why.

Information you provide

To use the app you sign in with a patient number and a one-time setup code issued by the clinic. Within the app you may record information about your care, such as your weight, fluid intake, exercise, calorie and nutrition entries, supplements, appointments and reminders, along with any notes or questions you choose to save. The app also keeps track of your progress through your care journey.

How your information is used

Your information is used only to support your care with the practice. It helps your care team understand your progress, and it lets the app release the right reading, trackers and prompts at the right stage of your journey. It is not used for marketing or advertising.

How it is stored and protected

Your information sits within the practice’s clinical system. Connections between the app and the practice are encrypted while in transit. Access on your device is tied to your patient number and your one-time setup code. After the first sign in, your device is securely linked so you do not need to enter the code again.

What we do not do

We do not sell your information. We do not share it with advertisers. We do not show third-party advertising in the app, and we do not use your information for any purpose outside your care.

Trusted service providers

Some features rely on trusted service providers to work, such as secure hosting. The optional food-photo and nutrition-estimate feature in the calorie tracker sends the photo or description you provide to a third-party processing service so it can estimate nutrition information. These providers process the information only to deliver the feature and do not use it for their own purposes.

Deleting your information

You can pause or delete the app at any time, with no effect on your clinical care. Removing the app from your device removes the local copy of your information. To request deletion of the information held for you in the app, contact the practice using the details below and we will action your request. See your privacy choices for the options available to you.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the date shown above. Where a change is significant, we will make it clear to patients.

Privacy enquiries

Questions about your privacy.

If you have questions about how your information is handled, or you would like to request deletion of your data, contact the practice during business hours. We will respond within one working day.
