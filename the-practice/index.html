The practice

Specialist care, excellence with continuity.

Meridian is a Melbourne-based practice focused on upper gastrointestinal, bariatric and metabolic health.

Care is delivered through an integrated team of surgeons, physicians and allied health clinicians, with structured follow-up over twelve months. Wherever possible, patients are reviewed by the same clinicians across assessment, treatment and recovery to support continuity and coordinated care.

The practice

Upper GI surgery, bariatric surgery and metabolic medicine, integrated by design.

Meridian combines FRACS-accredited surgical care with physician-led metabolic medicine within one coordinated multidisciplinary practice.

The practice holds adjunct and honorary academic appointments with the University of Melbourne and Monash University. Patients are assessed, treated and followed up within the same clinical ecosystem, supported by continuity of care and a unified clinical record.

Standard of care

Specialist surgical and metabolic care, delivered with continuity, coordination and considered detail.

Experienced multidisciplinary team

FRACS-accredited Upper GI and Bariatric surgeons working alongside a physician-led metabolic team, dietitians and psychologists. The practice maintains adjunct and honorary academic appointments with the University of Melbourne and Monash University.

Contemporary evidence-based care

Current laparoscopic and endoscopic techniques. Medical therapies, including GLP-1 treatment where clinically appropriate. Ongoing involvement in surgical and metabolic research, education and quality improvement.

Coordinated patient care

A single clinical record across the practice. Assessment, treatment and follow-up managed within one multidisciplinary structure, with clinical review occurring at the time decisions are made.

Considered practice environment

Private consulting suites, calm interiors and time for unhurried consultation. The patient experience has been designed to support comfort, clarity and confidence throughout care.

Continuity throughout treatment

Patients meet their treating surgeon early and continue care with a familiar clinical team across surgery and follow-up wherever possible. Care remains coordinated within the practice rather than across disconnected providers.

Care between appointments

The Meridian patient app extends support beyond the consulting room with education, recovery guidance, progress tracking and structured follow-up tools reviewed by the clinical team.

Where We Operate

A clinical environment designed around continuity of care.

Meridian is based at Brenan Place in Fitzroy, alongside specialist consulting, diagnostics and hospital services within a connected clinical precinct.

The location allows consultation, investigation, procedure and follow-up to occur within one coordinated environment, reducing fragmentation between providers and services.

Brenan Place was developed as a combined clinical and research facility, adjacent to St Vincent’s Hospital Melbourne and the Aikenhead Centre for Medical Discovery.
