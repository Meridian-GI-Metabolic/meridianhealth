The practice

A new standard for specialist care.

Meridian sets the clinical standard for upper gastrointestinal, bariatric and metabolic care in Melbourne.

Three surgeons, a physician-led metabolic team, and structured follow-up over twelve months. The same clinicians manage assessment, treatment and recovery.

Consulting suite

Brenan Place, Fitzroy.

The practice

FRACS-accredited surgeons. Physician-led metabolic medicine. One multidisciplinary file.

Meridian holds adjunct and honorary academic appointments at Monash University and the University of Melbourne. The practice was built for one purpose, to bring upper GI surgery, bariatric surgery and metabolic medicine into one clinical structure. The clinicians who assess you are the clinicians who treat you and follow up.

There is no transfer between disconnected providers. There is no waiting for a second referral. The clinical file moves with you.

Standard of care

The highest authority in our field. The latest medicine has to offer. The kind of comfort you would expect of five-star hospitality.

Most credible team

FRACS-accredited Upper GI and Bariatric surgeons, a physician-led metabolic team, dietitians and psychologists. Adjunct and honorary academic appointments at Monash University and the University of Melbourne.

Latest in medicine

Current laparoscopic and endoscopic technique. GLP-1 therapy where clinically appropriate. Practice connected to active surgical and metabolic research.

A new standard in patient care

Single clinical file. Same clinicians from assessment through twelve-month follow-up. Multidisciplinary review at the time of decision, not after.

Considered detail

Private consulting suites. Calm interiors. Time for unhurried consultation. The practice is built around the patient’s experience as much as the clinical work.

Continuity that puts patients at ease

You meet your surgeon at the first consultation, in theatre, and at every follow-up. Care does not pass through unnamed registrars or interchangeable staff.

Care between visits

The Meridian patient app extends clinical care into recovery. Guided protocols, education and trackers, all reviewed by your team.

Where we operate

Brenan Place. The quiet edge of Fitzroy.

A clinical and life sciences building, designed for specialist practice and research.

Meridian holds consulting suites at Brenan Place. Consulting, diagnostics and hospital services sit within the same precinct, allowing movement between consultation, procedure and follow-up to occur within one environment.

The building accommodates clinical practice alongside research. Patients access the same clinicians, in the same building, throughout the arc of care.

Adjacent to St Vincent’s Hospital and the Aikenhead Centre for Medical Discovery.

In practice

Patients enter through a clinical pathway. The pathway may be surgical, medical or both.

The decision is made from history, imaging, investigations and measured clinical need.
