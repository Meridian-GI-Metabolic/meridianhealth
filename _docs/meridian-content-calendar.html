Metabolic Dominance Campaign | 50 Posts Sequenced

Prepared June 2026 | For social media manager and content production team use

How to Use This Schedule

This document maps all 50 posts from the Meridian content bank to a sequenced 12-week publishing calendar. Each row in the weekly grids specifies the post number, title, pillar, format, platform, and the suggested publish day. The full copy and production notes for each post live in the companion document: meridian-content-50.html.

Cadence

Approximately 4 posts per week across 12 weeks. Weeks 6 and 12 carry 5 posts each to capitalise on mid-campaign momentum and close with a conversion push.

Pillar Balance

No pillar runs more than two consecutive posts. Authority and Education anchor the opening weeks; Patient Story and Competitive Positioning build through the middle; Culture and Performance close the campaign.

Format Alternation

Reels, carousels, and static posts are distributed so no two consecutive posts share the same format within a single week. Production load is spread: heavier formats cluster where lead time allows.

Suggested Publish Days

For weeks with 4 posts: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Saturday.

For weeks with 5 posts: Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday.

The specific day listed in each row is the recommended anchor. Adjust by one day in either direction based on team availability without disrupting the weekly pillar mix.

Priority Production Queue

Produce these pieces first. They carry the highest conversion potential or remain effective across the full campaign window regardless of where they appear in the feed. Getting these into post-production before the campaign launches removes the most significant risk to the schedule.
