A   Overview & Prerequisites

This document defines the five Stage 10 automation workflows for Brevo. Each workflow is a pathway-specific email nurture sequence triggered automatically when a clinician or administrator sets a patient's pathway attribute in Brevo following consultation.

Each sequence consists of three emails per track, sent at defined intervals. All five sequences are procedure-based — meaning they are initiated by a clinical decision, not a patient action, and the content of each email corresponds to the specific surgical or medical pathway the patient has been recommended.

These sequences are saved as configuration only. No automations are to be activated in Brevo until the Stage 10 Clinical Sign-Off Checklist is completed and signed by the reviewing clinician and the Director of Brand & Marketing. See Section C.

Prerequisites before building in Brevo

The following must be in place before any Stage 10 automation is configured in Brevo:

  • Contact attribute pathway (type: Text) created in Brevo → Contacts → Settings → Contact Attributes
  • Valid values for pathway attribute: sleeve bypass reflux_uppergi glp1_metabolic revision
  • All 15 email templates pushed to Brevo using scripts/brevo-push-templates.js (run locally from a whitelisted IP)
  • Clinical sign-off completed — see Section C and _docs/stage-10-clinical-signoff-checklist.html

Automation naming convention

Each automation in Brevo should be named exactly as shown in this document. Names are case-sensitive and are referenced in the activation checklist.

C   Activation Gate & Sign-Off Requirement

Do not activate any Stage 10 automation until all conditions below are met.

The following gate conditions must all be satisfied before any Stage 10 automation is switched from inactive to active in Brevo:

  1. All 15 Stage 10 email templates pushed to Brevo and confirmed present via scripts/brevo-verify-templates.js
  2. Clinical review of all 15 email bodies completed by a clinician or nurse lead — recorded in _docs/stage-10-clinical-signoff-checklist.html
  3. Any amendments required by the reviewer applied to both scripts/brevo-push-templates.js and _docs/stage-10-email-review.html
  4. Sign-off checklist signed (physically or by PDF signature) by Kathleen Twentyman, Director of Brand & Marketing
  5. Signed PDF uploaded and filing reference recorded in _docs/brevo-stage-10-setup-checklist.html
  6. Each automation in Brevo confirmed as inactive/draft before sign-off is finalised

Once all conditions are met, automations may be switched to active in Brevo one track at a time, starting with the highest-volume pathway. Confirm each activation with a test contact before proceeding to the next track.
