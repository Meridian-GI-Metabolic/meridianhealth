AHPRA Compliance Notes

  • No prescription medicine is named in this script. GLP-1 medications (including semaglutide and retatrutide) are prescription-only and cannot be promoted by name in advertising under TGA regulations. The script references "the injections" and "metabolic medicine" as patient-perspective language, not therapeutic promotion.
  • Retatrutide (Eli Lilly triple agonist) is not yet TGA-approved as of June 2026. It cannot be referenced as available or prescribed at Meridian. The patient's confusion about it is captured as a relatable audience experience only ("a new name you have never heard of"), not as a product Meridian offers.
  • All outcomes are qualified: "assessment required," "prescription not guaranteed," "individual outcomes vary." These must appear on the end frame of the video and in ad body copy.
  • The phrase "physician-assessed" is used throughout. This is both accurate and compliant, positioning medication as a clinical decision, not a consumer product.
  • Review the final cut with your AHPRA-registered physician before publishing. If Meta flags the ad for health content restrictions, the compliance disclaimer text on the end frame will support the review appeal.

Production Notes

  • Talent: Cast a woman who reads 38 to 45, not younger. The audience will immediately sense a performance if it skews aspirational. She should look capable and thoughtful, not distressed.
  • Tone: Understated confidence. This is not a before-and-after ad. The emotional register is: "I found the right answer, not a shortcut." No dramatic transformations, no weight reveal.
  • Setting: Home office or kitchen for the opening (relatable), Meridian-style clinical interior for the physician shot. Avoid generic stock hospital imagery.
  • Captions: Always on. At least 85 percent of Meta video is watched without sound. The on-screen text lines in this script carry the message independently.
  • Aspect ratios: Shoot 9:16 vertical for Reels and Stories, 1:1 for feed. The 30-second script works in both formats.
  • End frame: Meridian wordmark on cream (#f3f1ef), gold rule (#c2a55e), compliance text at 10px minimum. Button: "Start your assessment."
  • Audience targeting: Women 38 to 50, Melbourne metro, interests including health, nutrition, menopause, weight management. Exclude current enquiry leads via CRM custom audience.
