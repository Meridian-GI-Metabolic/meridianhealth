How to Use This Schedule

This document lists only the posts that require filming: all Reels and the one Story Series across the 12-week Metabolic Dominance Campaign. Carousel, Static, and graphic-only posts are excluded. Each weekly table shows the publish week, publish day, format, location, talent required, and estimated shoot time. Posts sharing the same location and talent are highlighted so the videographer can batch them into a single shoot day.

The Batch Shoot Day Guide at the end groups every filmable post into logical production blocks. Filming the correct batches in advance of each week's publish date is the critical path. Weeks 1 and 6 have the tightest deadlines and require the most pre-production lead time.
