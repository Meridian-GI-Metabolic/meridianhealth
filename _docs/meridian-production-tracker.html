How to Use This Tracker

This document tracks the production status of all 50 posts in the Metabolic Dominance Campaign. Posts are grouped by the week they are scheduled to publish, matching the meridian-content-calendar.html sequence. Update each row as the post moves through production. The tracker is interactive in the browser: use the status dropdown, tick the stage checkboxes, and fill in the producer and notes fields. Print when needed for team review.

Production Stages

Status Key
