What is included

Everything in the programme, upfront.

No hidden extras. No referrals to chase. The six elements of the Meridian metabolic programme, held in one annual fee.

  • Physician review, quarterlyFour consultations per year with the same AHPRA-registered consultant physician. Bloods reviewed, medication titrated, escalation managed.
  • GLP-1 therapy, where clinically indicatedPhysician-assessed and prescribed where appropriate. Titration, monitoring and side-effect management included. Prescription is not guaranteed and is determined by clinical assessment.
  • Dietitian sessionsScheduled with an Accredited Practising Dietitian, timed to your phase of care. Nutritional assessment, planning and ongoing adjustment.
  • Psychology supportAccess to a registered psychologist for behavioural and eating-related support. Scheduled when clinically indicated.
  • Meridian patient appYour clinical file, trackers, reading and direct messaging to your care team. Clinical team responds within one business day.
  • Shared care with your GPClinical letter sent to your GP after every review. Your whole care network stays informed.

How care is delivered

Video first. Clinic when needed.

Telehealth video reviews

The majority of ongoing care can occur from home. Specialist consultations, medication reviews, progress monitoring and treatment planning delivered via secure video.

  • Specialist physician consultations
  • Medication review and titration
  • Progress monitoring
  • Treatment planning

In clinic

Initial assessments and body composition analysis are conducted at Meridian in Fitzroy. Procedures requiring physical attendance are managed on-site.

  • Initial clinical assessment
  • Body composition analysis
  • Procedures where required
  • Quarterly physician reviews (where preferred)
The majority of ongoing care can occur from home. In-person attendance is required for the initial assessment and body composition scan.
Medicare rebates

Eligible consultations may attract Medicare rebates.

With a valid GP referral, specialist physician consultations may attract Medicare rebates, reducing your out-of-pocket costs. A referral is not required to enquire or attend an initial consultation.

  • 01 Specialist physician reviewsQuarterly consultations reviewed under the applicable MBS item number where a referral applies.
  • 02 Ongoing monitoringProgress reviews, blood work interpretation and medication titration may attract rebates under applicable items.
  • 03 Shared care with your GPClinical letters after every review keep your GP informed, supporting shared care arrangements and referral continuity.
Medication fulfilment

Medication delivered to your door.

Where medication is prescribed following clinical assessment, Meridian supports fulfilment through a partner pharmacy. Patients receive medication directly, without needing to visit a pharmacy.

Medication costs are separate to the programme fee and are arranged directly via pharmacy. PBS subsidies may apply to certain medications.

  • ✉Electronic prescriptions issued by your physician
  • 🏪Partner pharmacy dispensing
  • 📦Home delivery Australia-wide
  • 🔁Repeat prescription management
  • 💉Dose titration support from your physician
Programme options

Two ways to structure your care.

Both models include the same physician-led clinical programme. The difference is how medication is arranged. Your physician will discuss which model suits your situation at your first consultation.

Membership + medication separate

Programme fee covers clinical care. Medication is arranged and purchased separately through pharmacy.
