01 Upper GI Care

Upper GI Care

Care for patients with upper gastrointestinal conditions, including reflux, oesophageal, gastric and related gastrointestinal concerns.

Meridian provides clinical assessment, surgical care and ongoing patient support within one clinical setting.

02 Bariatric Surgery

Bariatric Surgery

Assessment and surgical care for patients considering bariatric surgery.

Meridian supports patients through consultation, assessment, procedure planning and follow-up care based on individual clinical needs.

03 Metabolic Care

Metabolic Care

Clinician-guided support for patients exploring non-surgical weight management and metabolic health care pathways.

Meridian provides assessment, education and ongoing clinical support based on individual needs.
