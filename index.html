How the practice operates

A clinician reads every enquiry. Yours is no exception.

Consultation includes review of imaging, endoscopy, bloods and prior history. A plan is documented at the time of assessment.

Surgical and medical care are coordinated within the same practice. There is no transfer between disconnected providers.

Follow-up is structured over twelve months where required.

When to contact Meridian

The questions our patients tend to ask first.

  • Persistent reflux or heartburn not controlled on medication
  • Known hiatus hernia or gallbladder disease
  • Referral for upper gastrointestinal surgery
  • Considering bariatric surgery
  • Medical weight management, including GLP-1 therapy
  • Second opinion for surgical or metabolic care
Membership

Care that holds, after the consultation ends.

Meridian Membership is the practice's physician-led programme for medical weight management and long-term metabolic health. Quarterly review with a consultant physician, GLP-1 therapy where clinically appropriate, dietetic and psychology input, and twelve-month structured follow-up.

Opened at the recommendation of a Meridian physician or surgeon. A clinical service, not a subscription product.
